Biography

A life shaped by faith, distance, and truth in Jesus Christ

Sergio is a young man who has lived more than eleven years outside his place of birth, walking through different cultures, seasons, and inner battles that shaped his identity and faith. What began as a journey for opportunity slowly became a journey for truth.

Leaving his homeland at a young age, Sergio faced loneliness, silence, and responsibility early in life. Living far from family and familiar surroundings forced him to grow, not only outwardly, but inwardly. In the absence of comfort, he learned discipline. In the absence of certainty, he learned faith.

Through personal struggles, moments of doubt, and seasons of isolation, Sergio encountered the truth of Jesus Christ not as religion, but as a living guide. Faith became the foundation that gave meaning to pain, direction to chaos, and purpose to solitude.
